Roosevelt is an RnB artist from Kansas City, Missouri whose musical style is a mix of sultry neosoul and urban RnB. His musical influences go as far back as Sam Cooke and The Temptations and as recent as Khalid, Daniel Greene, and H.E.R. Roosevelt approaches his music with a sort of boundless enthusiasm that only a RnB purist can provide. However, he is not afraid to take risks and it is evident is latest projects.

His first album Indecisively in love, available on iTunes, touches on love, lost, redemption and everything in between. His covers of top 100 RnB hits shows his ability to make any song his own. He is currently working on his sophomore album, title not yet released. Roosevelt has been singing all of his life.
